About SyncBranch

What is SyncBranch?

SyncBranch is a groundbreaking mobile app designed to uncover your unique musical identity, inspired by the mythological World Tree, Yggdrasil. By analyzing your Spotify listening habits, SyncBranch categorizes users into distinct music archetypes, like "Dreamer" or "Rhythm Seeker," and connects you with others who share similar tastes.

Spotify Integration

SyncBranch securely integrates with your Spotify account to analyze your playlists, listening history, and audio features like tempo, energy, and mood. This data powers a personalized music profile that reflects your unique habits, creating a deeper connection between you and your music.

Yggdrasil: The Mythical Inspiration

SyncBranch draws inspiration from Yggdrasil, the legendary Norse World Tree symbolizing connection and balance. Just as Yggdrasil bridges the nine worlds, SyncBranch connects people through shared musical experiences, fostering unity and discovery through the universal language of music.

Technologies We Use

Spotify API

SyncBranch leverages the power of the Spotify API to access real-time user data and analyze track features such as tempo, energy, and mood. This integration enables us to craft personalized music profiles, ensuring a unique and immersive experience for every user.

Flutter

Our mobile app is developed using Flutter, a robust UI toolkit that allows us to build visually stunning and high-performance applications. With Flutter, we deliver seamless experiences across both iOS and Android from a single, efficient codebase.

Python

The backbone of SyncBranch’s backend services is Python. Its versatility and efficiency empower us to perform complex data analysis, enable real-time communication with the Spotify API, and manage server-side operations with precision and speed.
